OUR HISTORY

Next Level1 Flooring Norfolk was established by two friends who share the same passion and commitment to the trade. With over 25 years of experience, you can rely on us to provide you with the highest quality of craftsmanship and products suited for the job in hand.

We specialise in all areas of contract and industrial floor coverings. With our expertise we can provide safety flooring in a wide range of sectors including wet room adaptations. We have the solutions to suit the needs of the client, also offering full preparation work ranging from latexing through to damp proof membrane.

We also provide domestic flooring varying from Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT), domestic vinyl, and carpets. With a wide selection of samples, we can offer in-home measure and quotation services, helping you choose your perfect flooring guaranteed to transform your house into a home.
